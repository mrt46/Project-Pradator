
================================================
PROJECT PREDATOR – RISK MANAGEMENT POLICY
================================================

Status: LOCKED

1. Every trade MUST have:
- Stop-loss
- Position size based on volatility

2. Kill-Switch Types:

2.1 GLOBAL KILL-SWITCH
Triggers:
- Total portfolio daily drawdown > 5%
- Systemic execution failure
- Infrastructure instability

Effect:
- ALL trading stops
- Cooldown period: minimum 12–24 hours

2.2 STRATEGY-LOCAL KILL
Triggers:
- Strategy violates its own risk envelope
- Abnormal losses
- Stop-loss discipline breach

Effect:
- Only that strategy is disabled
- System continues with other strategies

3. No strategy is allowed to:
- Endanger the entire system
- Bypass risk checks
- Bypass CRO veto

4. Risk ALWAYS overrides profit.
